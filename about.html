About Us

Welcome to UnderCity Games – your destination for fun, fast, and interactive browser-based puzzle games.

UnderCity Games is a modern online gaming platform focused on delivering lightweight and engaging games that can be played instantly without downloads or registration. Our goal is to make gaming simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone.

🎮 Who We Are

UnderCity Games is an independent game development platform specializing in Unity WebGL and HTML5 games. We design games with a strong focus on:

  • Smooth performance across all devices
  • Clean user experience with minimal friction
  • Creative visuals and modern animations

Our approach combines simple core mechanics with innovative design variations to create fresh and enjoyable gameplay experiences.

🧩 What We Do

We create and publish puzzle-based games that are easy to understand, challenging to master, and accessible across devices. Our games share a common gameplay foundation but are enhanced with different themes, characters, animations, and mechanics.

Current & Future Highlights:

  • Arrow Escape – a strategic puzzle game where players remove arrows in the correct sequence.
  • Future variations may include different concepts such as characters, environments, and interactive elements while maintaining engaging gameplay.

🌐 Play Anywhere

Our games are designed to be played instantly across multiple platforms, directly on our website or via Telegram Mini Apps. This ensures a seamless experience whether you are on mobile or desktop.
